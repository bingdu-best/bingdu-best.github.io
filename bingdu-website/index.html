04 / FAQ

The practical
questions.

Do we need to provide reference videos or ideas?+

No. Product information and brand assets are enough to start. If you already have winners, references or hypotheses, we can use them. If not, we research the market and build the testing plan.

How is this different from using an AI video tool?+

A generation tool creates clips. Bingdu delivers a creative system: research, concepts, scripts, production, quality standards, testing logic and continuous optimization—at scale.

How many videos can you produce?+

The system is designed to scale with demand—from a focused pilot to dozens of videos per day across multiple products. Volume, formats and delivery rhythm are set around your testing plan.

Can you manage the testing strategy too?+

Yes. You can tell us exactly what to test, or we can research the opportunity, define the schedule and use campaign results to decide what the next creative round should explore.
